About Stephy

Steph trained at a recognized culinary school and has completed official food handler safety training. She specializes in custom design work — sculpted cakes, character cakes, elegant wedding tiers and modern buttercream finishes. Every order starts with a conversation about your vision, dietary needs, and delivery logistics.
